How to make fries without oil
A Guide to help people with cooking
Summary
This is a dish that helps people to make fries in airfryer.

If you are allergic to potatoes, please do not try this dish.



Maximum Cooking Time: 1 hour

Cooking Process: Trime the Potatoes → Wash the Potatoes →

Cook the potatoes → Fry the Potatoes


Recipe

Ingredients
  • Potato
How to do?



  1. Peel the skin of the Potatoes (Depends on the amount of your potatoes)
  2. Cut the potatoes all into a similar size (Depends on the amount of your potatoes)
  3. Soak the trimmed potatoes in to a bowl of water (Depends on the amount of your potatoes)
      4. - To wash off the Starch
  4. Bring the water to boil and cook the potatoes (about 10 minutes)
  5. Place the cooked potatoes cool down on a plate (about 10 minutes)
      6. - Best to put cool the potatoes down with fan
  6. Peel the skin of the Potatoes
  7. Peel the skin of the Potatoes
  8. Peel the skin of the Potatoes
